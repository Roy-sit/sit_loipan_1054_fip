Your 5 Stars Dinning




Vacation memories are often formed by the taste of authentic local produce, feasts of fresh food, or exquisite dishes that appear like artworks on your plate. Excellent menu embracing this philosophy, and we've selected the very best fine dining experiences, here in Couples Resort.

With two acclaimed chefs—Diego Muñoz of Lima, Peru, and New York's John Fraser—overseeing its revamped restaurants and bars. Popular, the resort's signature restaurant, celebrates multicultural cuisine that brings together Incan, Spanish, Moorish, African, Italian, Chinese, and Japanese influences, while the Cantina & Pisco Bar will serve “live” ceviche, where guests can watch their dishes being expertly made. Louis, meanwhile, is a “global, fast-gourmet-food bazaar, bakery, and coffee and tea bar.”
